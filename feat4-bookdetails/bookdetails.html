Make changes to your book

Replace or view book text

Click the VIEW TEXT button to open your PDF text file to view.

Click the REPLACE TEXT below to upload and replace your book text PDF.

View Text Replace Text

Edit/replace or view your book cover

By clicking the EDIT COVER button below you will be taken to the cover generator where you can edit, view or replace the book cover.

Edit Cover

Change book retail price

Click the CHANGE PRICE button below to change your book’s retail price.

Your retail price must be at least $0.01 more than the wholesale price.

If TheBookPatch is generating an ISBN barcode for your book, you will be directed to the CREATE COVER page where you can change the price by clicking the Insert ISBN-13 and Generate Barcode button at the bottom of the CREATE COVER page.

Change Price

Change book title in bookstore

Clicking the CHANGE TITLE button below will take you to the cover generator where by changing the Book Title in the Edit Front Cover Book Title, you will change the title in the book store.

Change Title

Change author name in bookstore

Clicking the CHANGE AUTHOR NAME button below will take you to the cover generator where by changing the Author Name in the Edit Front Cover Author Name you will change the Author Name in the book store.

That will not affect your name as the account holder and is often used to enter a pen name or if you’re creating a book for someone who is not the account holder.

Change Author Name

Click the button below to get a shortened URL for your book that will take a user directly to your book in the bookstore. Upload or manage your e-book

To upload an eBook you first need to upload a printed book.

Click the button below and you will be taken to the eBook uploader page where you can upload a PDF, mobi and epub options.

You can upload one version, two or all three.

Upload/Manage Your eBook

Purchase or insert your ISBN

To publish your book in TheBookPatch you don’t have to have an ISBN.

To purchase an ISBN, click the PURCHASE ISBN button below.

  • Basic: $25 ISBN full distribution registration to Books In Print and U.S. ISBN Agency.
  • Enhanced: $40 ISBN full distribution and registration to Books In Print and U.S. ISBN Agency with full upload of PDF which enhances book search ability in all search engines.
  • Premium: $50 ISBN full distribution registration to Books In Print and U.S. ISBN Agency with full upload of PDF which enhances book search ability in all search engines and full front cover upload for all catalogues in books in print distribution.

To insert an existing ISBN to your book, click the INSERT ISBN button below.

You will be directed to the CREATE COVER page where you can insert the ISBN by clicking the Insert ISBN-13 and Generate Barcode button at the bottom of the CREATE COVER page.

Purchase ISBN Insert ISBN
